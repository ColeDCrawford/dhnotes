## Unit 4: Pipes and redirects

```
cat filename | less
man less
echo "hello world"
echo "hello world" > test.txt
echo "hello world 2" > test.txt
echo "hello world 3" >> test.txt
history
history | tail -5
history | tail -5 >> history-dump.txt
wc -l < history-dump.txt >
```
